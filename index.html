Experience

Talking about experience, I feel excited to share my journey as an IM student. Well, when the first time I applied for UPU , I didn't have much knowledge about this course and I just got a bit of information about it after reading a few blogs. I never expected that through this course, I got to learn a few subjects related to designing and editing stuff which was in semester 2 until semester 5 and it was a fun experience. Learning those subjects also made me think a few times about being a professional in editing and designing works in the future.

For me, semester 4 was the best semester so far because I got to learn about video editing using Adobe Premiere Pro for the first time in my entire life. Even though I used it for the first time, I can say that I am proud to look at the outcome of my work. I just want to share a quick story of what happened last semester when I was editing a video for my assignment. A few days before submission, I was editing a video and it was kind of 70% done. I thought I would just continue editing it until 2 am and continue for tomorrow. Suddenly, my laptop just restarted by itself before I got to save all the work. It was like having a restart schedule or maybe because I always put it in sleep mode for quite a long time and definitely all the work was gone and I had to redo it again.

Moreover, being an Exco Publicity of Peers Uitm Rembau club also made me have quite deep interest in doing creative works like creating posters, videos and arranging feed instagram of my club. I have already been involved in doing publicity work for about 2 years and I feel glad to show you guys some of my humble work that I did for my club and also for other projects. In doing creative work, your creativity is the most needed. I admit that I still need to learn more and have to practice often since I am still lacking in a few aspects such as the time consuming and lack of ideas.
